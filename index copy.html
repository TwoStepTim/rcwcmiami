Message from ED

Engagement refers to the active involvement and participation of individuals, communities, and organizations in child welfare initiatives. It encompasses various aspects, including families, caregivers, educators, case managers, child welfare professionals, and community members, all working together to ensure the safety and well-being of children within our system of care. Engagement makes a difference through empowering families, strengthening relationships, preventing recurrence and fostering community support.

As we embark on this journey together that leads to better outcomes for our children and families, my hope is that you gain the knowledge and tools that better equip you through this conference to enhance your productivity for the community we serve. Continued collaborative efforts strengthens the safety net around our children and provides resources to allow our families thrive in their greatest capacities. Thank you always for your dedication and commitment to child welfare. ENGAGEMENT MAKES A DIFFERENCE!

- Bryttany Stringer-Hollman, Executive Director

What time does the conference begin?

Conference begins at 9:00a. Registration begins at 8:00a.

Will there be breakfast and/or lunch?

Light refreshments will be served for breakfast. Box lunch will be provided to all registered participants.

How will I receive my certificate of participation?

All participants will receive their certificates via email post conference. Feedback form must be complete in order to receive certificate. In order to ensure you will receive your certificate, you must check-in with registration desk and complete feedback form.

When will I receive CLE credits?
